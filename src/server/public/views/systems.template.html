EAIR Systems

The system that we are demonstrating today is of the Enterprise Architecture Information Repository that was shown in the "EAIR today" portion of our presentation. This force chart displays the investments, products, data assets, systems, and activities that are associated with EAIR.

According to DHS, a system is a collection of "end products enabling products to achieve a given purpose." With EAIR as the system we are exploring, we want to provide users with a ready-made architecutre diagram of the products, investments, and activities that make EAIR what it is today.
